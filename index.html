Motivation

As a young adult living in Turkey's volatile economic environment, I often struggle to track my expenses and determine whether my spending is justified. My goal is to analyze my data to understand my habits, identify triggers for overspending, and determine if emotions play a role in my financial decisions.

Initial Hypothesis

I hypothesize that there are certain behavioral patterns in my spending habits that correlate with specific emotional states. For example, I might tend to overspend on leisure or comfort items during periods of stress or sadness.
